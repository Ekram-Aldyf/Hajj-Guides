Hajj Rituals Step-by-Step

  • Ihram: Intention and wearing the Ihram garment.
  • Tawaf: Seven rounds around the Kaaba.
  • Sa’i: Walking between Safa and Marwah.
  • Standing at Arafat: On the 9th of Dhul Hijjah.
  • Muzdalifah: Overnight stay after Arafat.
  • Stoning the Jamarat: Performed during Tashreeq days.
  • Shaving or trimming hair.
  • Tawaf Al-Ifadah.
  • Farewell Tawaf.

Pilgrim Rights

  • Clean and air-conditioned accommodation.
  • Appropriate daily meals.
  • Safe drinking water.
  • Religious and health guidance in your language.
  • Access to medical care when needed.

Pilgrim Duties

  • Follow instructions.
  • Respect companions.
  • Maintain cleanliness.
  • Stick to schedules and groups.

Religious Awareness

  • Keep your intention sincere for Allah.
  • Do not raise your voice during Tawaf or Sa’i.
  • Avoid arguments or harming others.

Health Awareness

  • Take recommended vaccines.
  • Carry an umbrella and wear a hat.
  • Stay hydrated.
  • Wash hands and wear a mask.
  • Rest between rituals.

Emergency Behavior

  • If lost: Contact your guide.
  • If tired: Sit and drink water.
  • In serious medical situations: Call 937 or 911 immediately.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can someone else throw stones on my behalf? Yes, if there is a valid excuse.
  • Is touching the Black Stone mandatory? No, it is Sunnah.
  • When do I shave or trim my hair? After stoning on Eid day.

Spiritual Advice

  • Consider every step an act of worship.
  • Don’t let the crowd distract you from your purpose.
  • Recite plenty of prayers and remembrance.
  • Hajj is a chance for forgiveness and renewal.
