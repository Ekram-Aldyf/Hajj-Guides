Roles and Responsibilities

  • Accompany pilgrims from arrival to departure.
  • Provide religious, service, and administrative support.
  • Ensure pilgrims' safety and comfort.
  • Submit daily comprehensive reports.
  • Communicate effectively with supervisors and authorities.

Dealing with Special Cases

  • Speak clearly to the elderly.
  • Personally accompany people with disabilities.
  • Ensure the availability of transportation and proper facilities.
  • Provide physical and emotional support as needed.

Reporting and Escalation Process

  • Assess and classify the case (emergency / non-emergency).
  • Use official forms to report.
  • Immediately report to the responsible supervisor.
  • Document and follow up on the case until resolved.

Daily Reports

  • Should include: number of pilgrims, health cases, notes, and complaints.
  • Submit by 10:00 PM in an organized format.
  • Must be written clearly and accurately.

Guide Conduct and Ethics


Be respectful in speech

Show humility and kindness

Listen actively and offer support

Field Scenarios and Training

  • Pilgrim with chest pain: Calm them and call emergency services.
  • Lost ID: Direct them to the nearest support center.
  • Argumentative pilgrim: Stay calm and explain instructions.
  • Language barrier: Use a translation app or multilingual guide.

Role in Awareness


Provide simplified guidance on rituals

Remind pilgrims of health practices

Engage spiritually and emotionally with the group

Guide Evaluation

  • Based on performance reports, pilgrim satisfaction, and supervisor feedback.
  • Considered for future Hajj seasons.
  • Evaluation includes: commitment, competence, kindness, and responsiveness.
